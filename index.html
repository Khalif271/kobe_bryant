The Late Kobe Bryant

The Black Mamba

Here's a time line of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia
  • 1996 - Drafted and traded - The iconic player was initially drafted to the Hornet's only to be traded soon after to the LA Lakers.
  • 1997 - Slam Dunk Contest Champion - He was only 18 at the time and did not make the team but was allowed to participate in the slam dunk competition which he won!
  • 1999-2002 - Phil Jackson arrives and the three-peat. This is third time that Phil Jackson has accomplished this feat. It is also the last time a team has been able to since.
  • 2004 -(2004) Fallout with Shaq. This was due to Shaq saying that Kobe was 'selfish' and a 'showoff' while Kobe was not pleased with Shaq's physical conditioning during games.
  • 2005 -Phil Jackson returns. The LA Lakers had this point ran through a number of different head coaches for various reasons and returned to hire a familiar face; Phil Jackson.
  • 2006 -Career-high 81 points. The Black Mamba goes on to score the most points in his career against the Toronto Raptors.
  • 2008 - Olympian Gold Medalist. The USA team was performing terribly until the Black Mamba arrived and ultimately winning them the Gold Medal.
  • 2008-2010 - Return to championship form. After the departure of Shaq, Kobe had yet to win a championship until these years. He had won two more, to claim a total of 5 rings.
  • 2012 -Final Olympic medal. At the age of 33 he was able to push the USA team to win his final Gold Medal.
  • 2020 - The Black Mamba with his 13 year old daughter have both passed away due to an unfortunate helicopter crash.
